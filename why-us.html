Why Us

Welcome to the home of the finest Model Railroads money can buy

Select Model Railroads discretely builds the highest quality model railroads for the most discriminating and distinguished modelers in O Scale (3 rail and 2 rail), Ho Scale, N Scale, S Scale and G Scale indoor. We also do narrow gauge in O Scale, S Scale & Ho Scale.

You reside in the best properties, vacation in the best resorts, drive the best cars...shouldn't you enjoy the best railroad you can have build? Select Model Railroads can make that happen for you. With over 100 combined years of experience our designers, modelers, artisans, electricians and carpenters can create your dream layout in our shop or on your location anywhere in the USA. Select Model Railroads also keeps all clients and their railroads strictly confidential so no one but us will ever know you had it professionally built.

From the design through the final ribbon cutting Select Model Railroads uses only the highest quality parts and materials in construction. Every step of the process we test, quality check, re-test and re-check to ensure you will have a lifetime of trouble free railroading once your layout is installed and given the final quality test.

From a small bedroom layout to a massive empire, Select Model Railroads is your choice for the finest design, most realistic scenery and most dependably constructed model railroad you can get.
